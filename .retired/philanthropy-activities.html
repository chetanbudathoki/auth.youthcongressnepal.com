BOOKS AND STATIONERY DISTRIBUTION PROGRAM

In any crisis, especially in the current pandemic, the citizens expect utmost honesty, efficiency, planning and transparency from the government. Lack of proper plans to cap the mounting cases of Covid- 19, smell of corruption in the government’s hands and other unjustifiable comments put by the ruling government and NCP co-chairman, led to a youth-led street protest ‘Enough is Enough’ in Kathmandu which snowballed nationally. YCon, having one of its major objectives to be a watchdog of the government activities to ensure good governance, actively participated in the protest.
