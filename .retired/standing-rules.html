Standing Rules

Youth Congress Nepal Standing Rules
  • Meetings, conventions, and all other programs shall be held at facilities that are accessible to a person with disabilities.
  • Youth Congress Nepal shall strive to be inclusive.
  • Communication shall be done with the proper use of technology.
  • Youth Congress Nepal shall abide by the Constitution of Nepal.
  • Women shall be treated equally.
  • Dalits, indigenous nationalities, persons with disabilities, minorities, marginalized communities, gender and sexual minorities shall be treated equally.
  • Activities shall be peaceful and without arms.
  • Economic support without consultation with the central committee are strictly prohibited.
  • Harassment, sexual violence, and domestic violence are strictly prohibited.
  • Activities shall be environment friendly.
